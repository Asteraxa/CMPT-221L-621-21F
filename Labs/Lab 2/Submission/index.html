Deckbuilding

Any good deck has a planeswalker at the forefront. Here are five of the best planeswalkers for beginners to try building around:

  1. Calix, Destiny's Hand
  2. Rowan, Fearless Sparkmage
  3. Mu Yanling, Celestial Wind
  4. Nahiri, Heir of the Ancients
  5. Basri, Devoted Paladin

Each of these planeswalkers is tied to an mechanic that is both easy to understand and well-supported by the game's developers!

I hope you enjoyed your stay, and remember to keep on building!
